# Community Workshop Registration MVP

## Summary

Create a small web app for a community center so residents can browse upcoming workshops, submit a registration request, and let staff review signups without relying on spreadsheets and email threads.

## Goals

- Make workshop information easy to understand on mobile and desktop.
- Let a resident register for a workshop in a few clear steps.
- Give staff a lightweight way to review registrations and confirm the attendee list.

## Audience

- local residents registering for community workshops
- community center staff reviewing registrations and managing confirmations

## Pages

- workshop list page
- workshop detail page
- registration request page
- staff registration review page

## Constraints

- keep the first release intentionally small
- no payment collection in the first MVP
- no automated email campaign tooling yet

## Success Criteria

- a resident can understand a workshop and submit a registration request quickly
- a staff member can review and confirm registrations in one place
- the backlog includes enough role-specific work for full V1 specialist review
