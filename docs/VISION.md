# Vision

## What AI Delivery Engine Is

AI Delivery Engine is a future orchestration layer for AI-assisted software delivery. It models AI roles as a delivery team:

- PO/PM
- UX/UI
- Front-end developer
- Back-end developer
- QA
- optional Tech Lead

The system is centered on a shared backlog instead of isolated prompts.

## Problem

AI tools are useful at individual tasks, but delivery often breaks down between tasks. Teams still need a way to:

- turn a vague brief into a structured backlog
- pass context from one role to the next
- record decisions and assumptions
- keep humans in control of scope and quality

## Product Direction

The product should help teams move from:

`idea or client brief`

to:

`reviewed backlog ready for implementation`

and later to:

`issues, implementation prompts, pull requests, and QA checks`

## Target Users

- freelancers handling discovery and delivery alone
- small agencies running repeatable client projects
- lean product teams that want AI support without full autonomy

## Design Principles

- backlog first, code later
- human approval at each critical handoff
- role-based outputs instead of generic chat transcripts
- simple contracts before automation
- vendor-neutral architecture

## Long-Term Outcome

In the long run, AI Delivery Engine should let a team start with a brief and progressively reach delivery artifacts with less manual coordination, while keeping review, traceability, and accountability.
