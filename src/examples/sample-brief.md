# Freelance Web and AI Studio Site

## Summary

Create a small marketing website for a freelance web and AI consultant. The first release should explain the offer clearly, build trust quickly, and convert interested visitors into contact requests.

## Goals

- Explain the freelance offer in simple language.
- Show credibility through case studies or proof points.
- Make it easy for a visitor to get in touch.

## Audience

- startup founders
- small business owners
- product teams needing fast delivery support

## Pages

- homepage
- services page
- case studies page
- contact page

## Constraints

- keep the first release intentionally small
- prefer content clarity over advanced features
- no CMS or complex integrations in the first pass

## Success Criteria

- visitors understand the offer in a few seconds
- the contact path is visible and simple
- the backlog is specific enough for a human delivery review
